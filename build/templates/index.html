A DallasNews Video Project

Seeing in Color

Race. A loaded word that causes tensions in our communities. Drives explosive news stories. Underlies how we are perceived, who we are. Yet it's uncomfortable to talk about. The Dallas Morning News wanted to help change that. We asked regular people about their experiences with race and diversity. These are their stories.
