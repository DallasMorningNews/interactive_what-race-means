Seeing in Color

This story is best experienced with your sound on

Race. A loaded word that causes tensions in our communities. Drives explosive news stories. Underlies how we are perceived, who we are. Yet we rarely talk about it. The Dallas Morning News wanted to help change that. We asked regular people about their experiences with race and diversity. These are their stories.
